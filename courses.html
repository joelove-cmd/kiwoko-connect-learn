Certificate in Nursing

Certificate
2.5 Years

Comprehensive nursing education preparing compassionate and skilled nurses for various healthcare settings.

Entry Requirements

  • •UACE with at least 2 principal passes
  • •UCE with at least 5 passes including English, Mathematics, and Sciences
  • •Must be at least 18 years old
